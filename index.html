Privacy & Policies

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: When you register or submit a report, we may collect your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location Data: For incident reporting, we collect location details (addresses, coordinates, etc.) to accurately identify where help is needed.

Incident Report Information

  • Reports: Details provided by users regarding stray animal sightings, including descriptions, urgency levels, and additional comments.
  • Media: Images or videos uploaded to support your report.

Adoption Listing Information

  • Animal Details: When listing an animal for adoption, you may provide information about the animal (e.g., type, breed, age, health status) along with photos.
  • Contact Information: Information provided by the person listing the animal to facilitate further communication.

  • Service Provision: To enable you to report incidents, list animals for adoption, and interact with local authorities, NGOs, or rescue teams.
  • Communication: To contact you regarding your reports or adoption inquiries, including follow-ups, confirmations, and updates.
  • Improvement & Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and trends, thereby improving our platform and services.
  • Legal and Safety Purpose: To comply with applicable laws, respond to legal processes, or protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and the public.

  • Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share necessary details with:

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., a subpoena, court order).
